Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026 · {{ brand.company }}

1. Who we are

{{ brand.name }} is a product of {{ brand.company }}, registered in India. Our cloud server and licensing system are hosted on Railway (USA). For any privacy-related concerns, contact us at {{ brand.support_email }}.

2. What data we collect

2a. When you purchase a license

We collect your email address for license key delivery and support. We do not store your payment card details — payment processing is handled entirely by Razorpay and subject to their privacy policy. We store: your email, license key (as an HMAC hash), plan tier, expiry date, and hardware machine_id (only after license activation).

2b. When you use the cloud C2 (PRO / Enterprise)

The desktop agent sends heartbeat data to our server every 10 seconds when enrolled in a tenant. This includes: machine hostname, operating system information, IP address, agent version, armed/unarmed state, and a hardware-derived machine_id. This data is scoped to your tenant and is never shared with other organisations.

2c. When you submit a contact form

We store your name, email, enquiry type, and message in our database to respond to your request. We do not use this for marketing without your explicit consent.

2d. What we do NOT collect

  • The contents of any monitored files
  • File hashes from your monitored folders (these stay local)
  • Keystrokes, browser activity, or screen content
  • Any data from your Google Drive — cloud backup uses OAuth scopes limited to FMSecure-created folders only

3. How we use your data

  • License delivery — your email is used to send your license key and any renewal notices
  • Fleet management — heartbeat data populates your organisation's C2 dashboard
  • Support — contact form submissions are used solely to respond to your enquiry
  • Security — machine_id is used to bind and transfer licenses, preventing unauthorised use

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data retention

  • License records: retained for the life of the subscription + 90 days after expiry
  • Agent telemetry: retained for 30 days of inactivity, then purged automatically
  • Alert records: retained for 90 days
  • Contact form submissions: retained for 12 months

5. Third-party services

  • Razorpay — payment processing (India). PCI-DSS compliant. Their privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • SendGrid (Twilio) — transactional email delivery. We pass only your email address and the message body.
  • Railway — cloud infrastructure hosting our server and PostgreSQL database. Data is stored in US-East region.
  • Google (OAuth) — optional cloud backup. Access is limited to a single application folder in your Drive.
  • abuse.ch MalwareBazaar — we send SHA-256 hashes of new/modified files for threat intelligence lookups. No file names or contents are sent.

6. Security measures

  • All server communication uses HTTPS (TLS 1.2+)
  • License keys are stored as HMAC-SHA256 hashes — not reversible
  • Tenant API keys are stored as SHA-256 hashes — plaintext shown only once at creation
  • Passwords are hashed with SHA-256 + custom salt
  • All local data files (logs, vault, records) are AES-256 encrypted using a hardware-bound KEK

7. Your rights

You have the right to: request a copy of your data, request deletion of your data, correct inaccurate data, and withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, email us at {{ brand.support_email }}. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Our web server uses a single session cookie (fmsecure_session or fms_tenant_session) to maintain your login state. These are HttpOnly, Secure, and expire after 24 hours. We do not use analytics cookies, tracking pixels, or advertising cookies.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be announced via email to active license holders. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
